About

Follow our progress on this website building.

  • 2022 Jan-Feb

    Our Motivation

    It all started with USC engineering class project under the guidance of Dr. Wu; we searched for a way to serve the community using skills obatined from the class. We learned there is not a well-established fire alert website while researching, which became our motivation to build one ourselves.

  • 2022 March

    Database Building

    We began to collect and clean the past fire and air quality data, and developed our web crawler system. Repositories and Firebase accounts were set up to store the resources. We were collecting our building blocks needed to build the website to show on public.

  • 2022 April

    Website Built

    The website theme was agreemd among team and fine tunings for the data were managed.

  • 2022 May

    Our contribution to community

    Finalized website is presented for the LA community to check wildfire and air qualities in real time, and for registered to get alerts around them!
